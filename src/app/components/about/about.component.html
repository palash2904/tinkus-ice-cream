Our Story

Born from a Love of
Real Milk

Growing up, Tinku Sharma watched his grandmother churn fresh malai kulfi every summer using milk straight from their family cow. That taste — rich, pure, and unforgettable — was something no commercial ice cream could replicate.

On 20 May 2025, Tinku turned that childhood memory into a mission: to bring 100% real milk ice cream back to people's lives. No shortcuts, no substitutes. Just pure milk, natural flavours, and honest ingredients.
